WE ARE OCA TUPI BRASIL

Oca Tupi begins its journey with a dream of winning different people, in different places, with a product of original and unmistakable taste. Our tapioca flour (hydrated cassava starch), is produced by hand, making the taste and quality unmatched. Our brazilian cheese bread has an amazing taste. Try out our escondidinhos (brazilian shepard's pie) and our caldinhos. Consume natural foods without preservatives. Oca Tupi Brasil. Original Recipe, Brazilian Flavour.
